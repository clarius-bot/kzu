#####  [Generate classes from XSD, now integrated with a guidance package!](http://www.clariusconsulting.net/blogs/kzu/archive/2005/07/22/XsdClassesGAT.aspx)

Generating classes from XSDs, and being able to customize the process has been a [favorite topic of mine for a while](http://msdn.microsoft.com/xml/default.aspx?pull=/library/en-us/dnxmlnet/html/xsdcodegen.asp). Now there's a Guidance Package that [Matias](http://blogs.southworks.net/matiaswoloski/) wrote for that very purpose, using the approach I proposed in [my MSDN article](http://msdn.microsoft.com/xml/default.aspx?pull=/library/en-us/dnxmlnet/html/xsdcodegen.asp). Read about the tool in [his blog entry](http://blogs.southworks.net/matiaswoloski/PermaLink,guid,b8853985-647c-4ea8-98e1-34c63ad15372.aspx).
