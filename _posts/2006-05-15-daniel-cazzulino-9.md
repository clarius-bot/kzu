#####  [Test Longhorn on VMWare](http://clariusconsulting.net/blogs/kzu/archive/2004/02/24/LonghornVMWare.aspx)

Longhorn doesn't work in current VMWare Workstation product (4.0). However, you can use the latest beta (4.5 RC1) which [adds (experimental) support for Longhorn](http://www.vmware.com/support/ws45/doc/releasenotes_beta.html) under a Windows 2003 Server VM. I've also downloaded it to fix a corruption I got on my VM disks after running Partition Magic, and it worked flawlessly.   
Download it from [this location](http://vmware-svca.www.conxion.com/secured/exp-ws/VMware-workstation-4.5.0-7174.exe) using the following registration information:  
UserName: workstation  
Password: experimental  
(information taken from [here](http://www.newhua.com/soft/2062.htm) \- not that I know japanese, but I figured it out ;)) 

Your current VMWare licence key will work. Enjoy!
