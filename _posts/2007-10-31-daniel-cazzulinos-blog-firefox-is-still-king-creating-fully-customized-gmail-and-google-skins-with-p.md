## Firefox is still king: creating fully customized GMail and Google skins with plain CSS 

Just get the [Stylish](http://userstyles.org/stylish/) add-on browse the huge library of community contributed styles to a myriad of sites. My customized Gmail hosted now looks much better (especially on my new small 12.1'' laptop :))

[![Gmail Hosted](https://web.archive.org/web/20081011143825im_/http://www.clariusconsulting.net/images/blogs/kzu/asdf_12C/GmailHosted_thumb.png)](http://www.clariusconsulting.net/images/blogs/kzu/asdf_12C/GmailHosted.png)

[![Gmail Hosted-Compose](https://web.archive.org/web/20081011143825im_/http://www.clariusconsulting.net/images/blogs/kzu/asdf_12C/GmailHostedCompose_thumb.png)](http://www.clariusconsulting.net/images/blogs/kzu/asdf_12C/GmailHostedCompose.png)
