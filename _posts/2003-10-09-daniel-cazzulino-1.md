## El Crimen del Padre Amaro 

[This movie](http://us.imdb.com/title/tt0313196/) was nominated for the [best foreign languave movie Oscar](http://www.oscar.com/nominees/nom_32075.html) award this year. It didn't won, but I'm surprised it even got that far. It's a very good film, but with a hard topic (the Church and the humans that represent it) and a crude way of telling the story. It's beatifully told, don't misunderstand me, but it surely pissed-off many. I was left with general sad feeling and contradictory thoughts about the characters.  
Highly recommended, and a big congratulation for mexican cinematography, which is getting better and better (I have good memories of [Amores Perros](http://us.imdb.com/title/tt0245712/) and [Y tu mama tambien](http://us.imdb.com/title/tt0245574/), not surprisingly with the same main actor, the young and talentous [Gael Garc√≠a Bernal](http://us.imdb.com/name/nm0305558/)).  
I also remember [La Ley de Herodes](http://us.imdb.com/title/tt0221344/), a hilarious and sardonic movie about the politicians world.
