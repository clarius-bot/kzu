## XQuery AND XSLT 2.0 WILL be available for .NET v2.0!!! 

[Altova ](http://www.altova.com/)announced a royaltee-free distribution of [AltovaXML ](http://www.altova.com/altovaxml.html)engine complete with .NET interfaces. It supports XQuery 1.0 and XSLT 2.0 (with or without schema support). Excelent news for .NET developers!!!  
  
[Via TSS](http://www.theserverside.net/news/thread.tss?thread_id=35459) (thanks [Paul](http://weblogs.asp.net/paulballard/)!)
